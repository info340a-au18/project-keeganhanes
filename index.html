Our Vision

The Issue

The U-District is a very busy and populated area of Seattle with limited parking. More than half of UW’s students live off campus and many of those students have cars. When the place you usually park is completely full or you’re parking somewhere you have never been before it can be frustrating to find a spot and even cause you to be late to where you’re going.

And that's why we made UD-Parking! Our site provides an easy and convenient way to find the best places to park and how much it’s going to cost, saving you your time and money.

Our Inspiration

“RYD” is a start up in Vancouver WA that acts like an Uber for employees at various businesses around downtown. The service is free due to an agreement with the city to fully fund it. RYD’s vision is not only to help transport employees to and from work but to also take the general public where they need to go, free of charge. This idea came about after several companies made complaints about how limited parking was for their employees.

Although incorporating a service similar to RYD in Seattle would be much more difficult considering the cities size, we believe there are still ways to help the situation. RYD has inspired us to act upon the growing parking issue within the U-District and in the future, all of Seattle.
